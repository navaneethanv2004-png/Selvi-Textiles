About Selvi Textiles

Empowering Healthcare with Quality Textiles

Our Story

Established with a vision to revolutionize medical supplies, Selvi Textiles has grown into a leading manufacturer and supplier of medical textile products. We specialize in producing premium quality bandages, cotton rolls, gauze rolls, and sterile swabs that serve as the backbone for daily clinical, surgical, and hygiene procedures.

With years of expertise in the textile industry, we understand that medical products demand absolute compliance with safety and hygiene standards. Our manufacturing unit employs advanced machinery and rigorously monitored processes to ensure every product we pack is 100% safe, sterile, and highly effective.

Our Mission

To deliver flawless, hygienic, and affordable medical textiles to the healthcare industry, contributing to better patient care and medical safety globally. We believe in uninterrupted supply, transparent communication, and uncompromised quality.
